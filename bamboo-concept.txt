Bamboo

Bamboo is a kind of grass. Its stem is hard and strong, like the wood from trees. But bamboo grows back quickly when it is cut down. Most trees do not. That is why bamboo is a popular material.

People use bamboo to build many things. Floors can be made from bamboo boards. People can make soft rugs from bamboo threads. Even chairs and tables can be made from bamboo. 

Spoons and bowls can be made from bamboo. A garden fence can be made from bamboo, too. Bamboo is a very useful plant material!
