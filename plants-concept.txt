Plants

Plants grow in many places.
They can be big.
They can be small.
Plants grow in cold lands.
They grow in hot lands.

All plants need sunlight.
Sunlight helps them grow.
All plants need water. 
Water helps them grow.

All plants have parts.
Flowers make fruit and seeds.
Fruits hold seeds.
Leaves get sunlight and make food.
Stems carry water from the roots to leaves.
Roots take in water.

Roots carry water to stems.
Stems carry water to leaves. 
Leaves take in sunlight. 

Plants make flowers. 
Flowers make seeds. 
Plants grow from seeds. 

Plants are nice to look at. 
Some plants are good to eat.
