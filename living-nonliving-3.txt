Living things can grow. 
Living things can use senses. 
Living things can have babies. 
Many living things move on their own. 

Nonliving things do not grow. 
Nonliving things do not use senses. 
Nonliving things do not have babies. 
Nonliving things do not move on their own. 

Living things breathe and consume food and water. Living things also excrete air, water, and food that can't be used. 

Living things need space to live. People and animals need shelter. Shelter keeps them warm, dry, and safe. 

Plants and animals are living things. You are a living thing too.
