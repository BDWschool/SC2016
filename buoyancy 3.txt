Water is fluid. Other things can pass through it. 

A balloon floats. When something stays on the surface of the water, we say it floats.

The fish hovers. If something stays in the middle of the water and doesn't sink or float, we say it hovers. 

Some things like eggs sink in fresh water and float in salt water. The salt water is more dense and can keep the thing on the surface better.
