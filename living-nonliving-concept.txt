## Nonliving Things and Living Things 

Living things can grow. 
Living things can use senses. 
Living things can have babies. 
Many living things move on their own. 

Nonliving things do not grow. 
Nonliving things do not use senses. 
Nonliving things do not have babies. 
Nonliving things do not move on their own. 

Plants are living things. Animals are living things. You are a living thing too. 

All living things have needs. 

Living things need to breathe. Living things need to consume food and water. Living things also excrete air, water, and food that can't be used. 

Living things need space to live. Plants need space to grow. People and animals need shelter. Shelter keeps animals and people warm, dry, and safe.
